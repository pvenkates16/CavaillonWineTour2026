Alarm Instructions

Two routines — one for leaving, one for bedtime

Leaving the house — all of you
1Make sure all cars and people have left the property
2Press button 2 on the remote → blue LED lights up → you can leave (gate auto-closes in 50 sec)
3On return: press button 1 first (blue LED disappears), then press button 2 to open the gate
⚠ Opening the gate before disabling the alarm will trigger it. You have 60 seconds to cancel.
Going to bed — mandatory every night
1Find the key box in the back kitchen
2Insert key for at least 2 seconds without turning — green light comes on, then remove key
3Morning: same procedure to switch off (green light goes out)
ℹA flashing red light is normal — ignore it, it clears on its own
Remote monitoring
If a wrong move triggers a SOTEL call (phone in the living room), give them your alarm code (provided on arrival). Call Isabelle first if unsure. Unannounced SOTEL visit costs €79.
